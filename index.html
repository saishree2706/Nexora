Who We Are

Engineering Excellence Rooted in
Silicon Beach City_

Nexora Global Engineering Solutions is a specialized multidisciplinary engineering organization formed by experienced professionals with deep expertise in plant engineering — across both offshore and onshore systems.

With a strong foundation in Silicon Beach City, we combine regional technical strength with global engineering practices to deliver high-quality, cost-efficient solutions.

Vision

To become a globally trusted engineering partner delivering innovative and sustainable solutions.

Mission

High-quality, cost-efficient engineering services through technical excellence and collaboration.

What We Do

Core Engineering
Services

Precision engineering across the full project lifecycle — from concept to commissioning.

01⚙️

Process

P&IDs, hydraulic calculations, line sizing, HAZOP study, feasibility study, material selections, equipment sizing & calculations, MR & TBE, and process optimization for industrial plants.

02🔧

Mechanical Equipment

Vessel calculations of static equipment and pressure vessels, material handling, support, and auxiliary systems.

03🛡️

HSE

HAZOP and HAZID study, compliance and standards such as OSHA, OISD, and ISO for standard practices.

04🔩

Piping

3D piping plot plan, conceptual study, layout, BOQs, isometrics, stress analysis, pipe support design, material specifications & as-builts.

05🏗️

Civil & Structural

Racks, foundations, process structure, offshore platform design and analysis.

06⚡

Electrical

SLDs, load list, HAC classifications, cable scheduling and routing layout of cables and cable trays with supports.

07📡

Instrumentation & Telecommunication

Transmitters, control system, actuator selection, I/O schedule, loop drawing, and F&G system design.

08📐

Pipe Stress / Structural Analysis

Static analysis, dynamic analysis, thermal, wind and seismic analysis. Pipe stress mark-ups for critical areas.
